The Business Corridor Between Asheville & Hendersonville

Fletcher sits right in the middle — the practical center of the region, home to Asheville Regional Airport and the retail and industrial corridor along Airport Road and I-26. It’s where the area shops, works, and increasingly, where the area is growing. As Asheville housing has tightened, Fletcher has become one of the fastest-growing communities in the region for both businesses and families.

Carolina Blinds has worked across the Fletcher area since 1992 — and unlike a one-size product line, we handle both sides of this market: the offices, storefronts, restaurants, and medical suites along the commercial corridor, and the established and new-construction homes filling in around them.

We’re minutes away from both directions: our Hendersonville showroom is a short drive south on US-25, and our Weaverville showroom is just north past Asheville. Or we come to you — free consultations for any Fletcher business or home.
