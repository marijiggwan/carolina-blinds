Window Treatments for Burnsville & Mountain Air

Burnsville is home to Mountain Air — one of WNC’s premier gated mountain communities. Homes here feature expansive windows, dramatic elevation views, and architectural details that demand custom window treatment solutions.

Carolina Blinds has served the Burnsville area for years, including numerous installations in Mountain Air. We understand the specific challenges of high-altitude homes — intense sun exposure, temperature extremes, and the desire to frame those 360-degree mountain views rather than block them.

Our Weaverville showroom is the closest location, an easy drive down the parkway. We also offer free in-home consultations where we bring samples directly to your home or business, measure every window individually, and provide a detailed estimate within 2–3 days.
