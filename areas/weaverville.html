Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from Weaverville homeowners about window treatments.

Custom window treatment costs vary based on window size, product type, and features like motorization. Blinds typically start around $150–400 per window, shades from $200–600, and plantation shutters from $350–800+. Motorized options add $150–300 per window. We offer options for every budget across our Hunter Douglas, Alta, and Eclipse lines. The best way to get accurate pricing is to schedule a free in-home consultation — we measure your specific windows and provide a detailed estimate within 2–3 days with no obligation.

WNC mountain homes have unique needs we understand well after 33 years here. Large windows with mountain views benefit from top-down/bottom-up shades that preserve the view while controlling light and privacy. Cellular (honeycomb) shades provide excellent insulation — critical for our cold winters and helpful with energy costs. For moisture-prone areas like bathrooms and kitchens, we recommend alternative wood blinds or moisture-resistant shutters rather than natural wood. Solar shades are ideal for sun-facing windows with intense afternoon exposure. Our team will assess your home's specific orientation, elevation, and window types during a free consultation.
