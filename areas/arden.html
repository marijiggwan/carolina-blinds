Window Treatments for Arden’s Family Homes

Just south of Asheville, Arden is one of WNC’s most established residential areas — a mix of settled family neighborhoods, upscale communities like Biltmore Park and Walnut Cove, and a steady run of new construction filling in along the I-26 corridor. These are homes where window treatments matter for everyday living: light, privacy, and comfort across living spaces, bedrooms, and growing families’ rooms.

Carolina Blinds has served Arden since 1992. Many of its homes — Craftsman, Tudor, Colonial, and the open floor plans built through the 2000s and since — feature tall great-room windows, banks of windows facing the Blue Ridge, and the kind of light-control challenges we solve every day.

We bring the showroom to you. Free in-home consultations across every Arden neighborhood, with samples you can hold up against your own walls, floors, and light.

Serving Every Arden Neighborhood

From established communities to brand-new builds, we know Arden’s homes and what their windows ask for.

Biltmore Park & Walnut Cove

Upscale homes with open floor plans, large windows, and mountain views. Solar and sheer shades preserve the view while cutting glare; motorization handles the tall and banked windows these homes are known for.

Royal Pines & Established Neighborhoods

Family homes where it’s often time to refresh or replace aging blinds. Durable, easy-to-operate options for busy households, with child-safe cordless lifts for kids’ rooms.

New Construction

Building or recently moved in along the I-26 corridor? We coordinate with your timeline, measure as the home is finished, and can install a whole house in a single visit.

Energy & Comfort

Cellular honeycomb shades add insulation for WNC winters, and top-down/bottom-up options give privacy at eye level while keeping the view above — ideal for bedrooms and street-facing rooms.
