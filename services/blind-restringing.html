Don't Replace Your Blinds — Restring Them

If your blinds won't go up, won't go down, or hang unevenly, the problem is almost always a broken or worn cord — not a broken blind. Restringing restores full function at a fraction of the cost of replacement.

Carolina Blinds restrings most brands of horizontal blinds including wood, alternative wood, and aluminum. We can often complete the repair during a single service visit to your home.

Signs You Need Restringing

Blinds won't raise or lower evenly, cords are frayed or visibly damaged, slats hang at an angle, the lift cord pulled through the bottom rail, or the blind is stuck in one position.

Pricing

Our service visit fee is $60 (covers the trip to your home). Restringing cost depends on blind width and type — we'll provide an estimate before proceeding. Most restringing jobs are significantly less expensive than buying new blinds.
