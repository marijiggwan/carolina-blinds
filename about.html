33 Years of Beautifying WNC Homes

Wayne Patterson opened Carolina Blinds in 1992 when the mountains here in Western North Carolina were very different. Starting as a small manufacturer of blinds on 7th Avenue East in Hendersonville, the company has grown to two showroom gallery locations — but the commitment to personal service hasn't changed.

Today, Carolina Blinds is one of Western North Carolina's first and most experienced full-service custom window covering companies. Our Hendersonville showroom behind Lowe's and our Weaverville location near Goodwill together feature over 150 functional window covering displays you can see, touch, and operate.

As an authorized Hunter Douglas Centurion Gallery dealer, we carry the finest window treatment brands alongside Alta, Eclipse, and other premium lines. But what truly sets us apart is our people.

No Subcontractors — Ever

Our installers are Carolina Blinds employees — trained, experienced, and part of your community. They're not contract workers cycling through. They take pride in precision work because their name is on it. Every installation includes our quality control process and free in-home service for one full year.

Living Wage Certified

We believe in taking care of our team, which is why Carolina Blinds is Living Wage Certified. When you choose us, you're supporting a local business that invests in its people and its community.
