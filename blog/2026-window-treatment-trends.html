2026 Window Treatment Trends for Asheville and Hendersonville

As we enter 2026, window treatment design continues to evolve. Here in Western North Carolina, homeowners are embracing trends that blend mountain aesthetics with modern technology.

1. Smart Home Motorization

The biggest trend: Hunter Douglas PowerView® automation. Control every shade from your phone, set schedules, integrate with Alexa/Google/HomeKit. Especially popular for hard-to-reach high windows, skylights, and screened porches.

2. Natural Materials & Textures

Woven wood shades, bamboo, jute, and natural grass materials — a perfect match for WNC's mountain aesthetic. Organic warmth that complements both rustic and contemporary designs.

3. Energy Efficiency as Standard

Cellular honeycomb shades have moved from “upgrade” to “standard.” Triple-cell construction provides maximum insulation — critical during WNC winters.

4. Sustainability & Eco-Conscious Choices

More customers asking about sustainable materials and recyclable components. Choosing quality treatments that last 20+ years is itself an act of sustainability.

5. Layered Window Treatments

Combining sheers with blackout shades, or pairing Roman shades with side panels. Creates depth and versatility for different times of day and seasons.
