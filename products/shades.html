Why Shades Are Perfect for WNC Mountain Homes

Western North Carolina’s mountain climate creates unique challenges: cold winters, intense summer sun, elevation-driven temperature swings, and the desire to preserve those stunning views. Shades are the most solution-driven window treatment category because each type addresses specific problems.

Cellular honeycomb shades are our top recommendation for insulation — their air pockets work like double-pane windows, trapping heat in winter and blocking it in summer. Homeowners regularly report noticeable reductions in heating and cooling costs after installation.

For homes with large view windows, solar shades are ideal — they cut glare and UV damage to furnishings while maintaining the mountain panorama. And top-down/bottom-up shades give you the best of both worlds: privacy at eye level with the mountain view preserved above.

Every shade we sell comes with professional measurement and installation by our own team — no subcontractors. Free in-home service for one year.
