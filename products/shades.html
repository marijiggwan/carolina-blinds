Types of Shades We Carry

Shades are the most versatile window treatment category. Unlike blinds and shutters, they don't have slats — they're solution-driven, designed to solve specific problems like energy efficiency, privacy, light control, and glare protection.

Cellular (Honeycomb) Shades

Made from cells designed to expand to cover a window and collapse when opened. Single, double, or triple cell construction provides increasing levels of insulation. Our top recommendation for WNC mountain homes — the insulating air pockets can reduce energy costs by up to 40%.

Why Shades Are Perfect for WNC Mountain Homes

WNC's mountain climate creates unique challenges: cold winters, intense summer sun, elevation-driven temperature swings, and the desire to preserve stunning views. Shades are the most solution-driven window treatment category because each type addresses specific problems.

Cellular honeycomb shades are our top recommendation for insulation — their air pockets work like double-pane windows. Homeowners regularly report noticeable reductions in heating and cooling costs.

For homes with large view windows, solar shades are ideal — they cut glare and UV damage while maintaining the mountain panorama. Top-down/bottom-up shades give you privacy at eye level with the view preserved above.

All shades are available cordless and motorized. Brands we carry include Hunter Douglas, Alta, and Eclipse — visit our showrooms to compare them side by side.
