Why Homeowners Choose Shutters

Shutters are the only window treatment that adds permanent architectural value to your home. They’re not an accessory — they become part of the home itself. That’s why shutters consistently rank as the highest-return window treatment investment.

In WNC, shutters are especially popular because they handle our variable climate beautifully. They provide excellent insulation, precise light control through adjustable louvers, and a timeless aesthetic that works with everything from mountain rustic to contemporary design.

Our most popular choice in Hendersonville and Asheville is the classic plantation shutter with 4.5” louvers — wide enough to maximize views and light when open, with complete privacy when closed. For homes with unique architectural windows (arches, angles, circles), custom-shaped shutters turn challenging windows into design features.

Every shutter we install is custom-measured and installed by our own team. No subcontractors. Free in-home service for one year.
