Exterior Installation

We install weatherproof exterior shades on large windows to protect against sun damage and glare, and to maintain cooler interior temperatures during warmer months. For homes with south-facing or west-facing walls of glass, exterior shades make a dramatic difference in comfort and energy costs.

Outdoor Living Spaces & Porches

Have a separate outdoor patio or attached porch? Extend the good times long into the cooler months with exterior shades. Screened porches are one of WNC's favorite living spaces — exterior shades make them usable for more of the year.

Eclipse E-Zip Shades are engineered for exterior use and can handle significant wind loads. Exterior shades can lower covered area temperatures by up to 20 degrees. They block 97% of UV rays before that energy hits your glass, which is far more effective than interior treatments alone. This also protects furniture from sun damage and reduces cooling costs.
