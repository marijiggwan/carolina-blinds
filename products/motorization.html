Why Motorization Makes Sense for WNC Homes

Mountain terrain means multi-level construction — basement/terrace, main floor, and often a second story that's really the third floor. That means high windows, skylights, and hard-to-reach spots everywhere. Motorized shades solve this completely. Bedrooms, theater rooms, and screened porches are other popular applications.

Exterior E-Zip Shades

Mountain life means outdoor life. Extend your living space with heavy-duty exterior shades.

Exterior installation of weatherproof shades protects against sun damage, glare, and helps maintain cooler interior temperatures during warmer months. For outdoor living spaces and screened porches, exterior shades extend the good times long into the cooler months.

Benefits of Exterior Shades:

Reduce bugs and critters. Protect interior furniture from sun damage. Protect outdoor furniture from weather. Maintain cooler temperatures (can lower temps up to 20 degrees). Block 97% of UV rays. Help block rain and wind.
