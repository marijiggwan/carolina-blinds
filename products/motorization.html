Why Motorization Makes Sense for WNC Homes

Western North Carolina’s mountain terrain means homes are often built on slopes with multi-level construction — basement or terrace level, main floor living, and a second story that’s really a third floor. That means high windows, skylights, and hard-to-reach spots everywhere.

Motorized window treatments solve this completely. High angular windows that let in gorgeous morning light? Set them to close automatically when afternoon sun gets intense. Skylights 20 feet up? Control them from your phone. Screened porch with E-Zip shades? Open and close with a button — no climbing, no reaching.

Bedrooms are another popular use — transition from fully open to complete blackout at the touch of a button or on a schedule. Theater rooms, nurseries, and home offices all benefit from precise, automated light control.

We specialize in motorization and have live PowerView demonstrations in both showrooms. Visit us to see it in action before you decide.
