Why Choose Motorization?

Motorization isn’t a separate product — it’s an option available on virtually every blind, shade, and shutter we carry. Here’s why WNC homeowners love it:

One touch, every shade. Operate all your window treatments at once from your phone, remote, or voice assistant. Set schedules so shades open at sunrise and close at sunset automatically.
Place furniture anywhere. No cords mean no need to leave space for manual access. Arrange your room however you want.
No visible cords. Cleaner look, safer for children and pets, and no tangled cords hanging down your walls.
Reach every window. No need to stretch to the top of tall windows, skylights, or hard-to-reach spots. Especially valuable in WNC’s multi-level mountain homes.
Sunrooms and walls of windows. When you have 8, 10, or 15 windows in one room, motorization is the only practical way to control them all.
Modern feel, fun factor. There’s something deeply satisfying about pressing a button and watching an entire room transform. Guests love it.
